Most visitors arrive on a phone. This starter keeps the hero photo visible on
mobile — stacked below the title — instead of hiding it the way the upstream
theme does.
<!--more-->

## A face builds trust

For personal-brand sites, the photo is a conversion driver, so it should never
disappear on small screens. The hero stacks vertically: title first, photo
below.

## Test at 375px

When you customise the site, check the layout at a 375px viewport width to make
sure your hero image still looks great on the smallest common phones.
