Welcome to your new site. This sample post shows how an article looks — from the
teaser that appears on the blog list, through to the full body you are reading now.
<!--more-->

## Make it yours

Edit the front matter at the top of this file to change the title, date and
`feature_image`. Everything below the `<!--more-->` marker becomes the full
article; everything above it is used as the teaser on the blog list page.

## Writing posts

Create a new post by adding a Markdown file to `content/blog/`, or run
`hugo new blog/my-post.md` to scaffold one from the archetype. Posts are listed
newest-first automatically.
